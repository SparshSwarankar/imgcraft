Watermarking Best Practices

Placement

Avoid corners where watermarks can be easily cropped out. Place them near the center or over complex details for better protection.

Transparency

Use 30-50% opacity. Your watermark should be visible enough to deter theft but subtle enough not to ruin the viewing experience.
